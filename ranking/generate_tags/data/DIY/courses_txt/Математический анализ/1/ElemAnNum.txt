ELEMENTARY NUMBER THEORY
ERIC T. MORTENSON
1. Course description
We will not assume knowledge of complex analysis. Sources for lectures will be the
notes from courses run by Bob Vaughan at Penn State University and Imperial College
London.
Reference texts:
(1) G. E. Andrews, Number Theory,
(2) H. Davenport, Multiplicative Number Theory,
(3) L.-K. Hua, Introduction to Number Theory,
(4) I. Niven, H. Zuckerman, H. Montgomery, An Introduction to the Theory of Numbers,
(5) I. M. Vinogradov, The Method of Trigonometrical Sums in the Theory of Numbers..
